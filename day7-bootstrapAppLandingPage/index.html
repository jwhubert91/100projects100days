MyApp Pay

In China, users who have provided bank account information may use the app to pay bills, order goods and services, transfer money to other users, and pay in stores if the stores have MyApp payment option. Vetted third parties, known as "official accounts", offer these services by developing lightweight "apps within the app".

B2C Mode

MyApp's main profit center. This mode has been used by many hospitals, banks, fashion brands, internet companies and personal blogs because the Official Account can access online payment, location sharing, voice messages, mini-games, etc.

Last updated 3 days ago

Surveillance

MyApp operates from China under Chinese law, which includes strong censorship provisions and interception protocols. MyApp can access and expose the text messages, contact books, and location histories of its users. Due to MyApp's popularity, the Chinese government uses MyApp as a data source to conduct mass surveillance in China.
